# TC-006 - Add to cart one or more items

**ID:** TC-006

**Title:** Verify that user can add one or more items to the cart from the product listing page  

**Preconditions:** 
- User is logged in successfully
- Inventory page load and products are visible

**Steps:** 
1. Navigate to the Products page (`/inventory.html`).  
2. Click on the **Add to Cart** button for any item.  
3. (Optional) Click on the product image to open the product details page, then click **Add to Cart**.  
4. Observe the cart icon on the top right.  

**Expected Result:** 
- Clicking on the product image opens the detailed product view.  
- Clicking **Add to Cart** adds the selected product to the shopping cart.  
- The cart icon in the top-right corner updates with the correct item count.  


**Actual Result:**  
- Product image opens in detailed view.  
- **Add to Cart** button works correctly and updates the cart icon.

**status:** Pass
